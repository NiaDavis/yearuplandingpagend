About Year Up

Year Up is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underserved young adults to succeed in professional careers and higher education. Through a one-year, intensive training program, Year Up provides young people aged 18-29 with a combination of hands-on skill development, coursework eligible for college credit, and corporate internships. Since its inception in 2000, Year Up has served thousands of young adults and has demonstrated a commitment to closing the Opportunity Divide that exists in our society. The success of Year Up highlights the potential of young adults to thrive when given the right opportunities and support. - Nia Davis
